--- layout: singleBigElement bannertitle: Japanese Garden scene bannersubtitle: I just wanted to make something pretty title: Sebastien Decuyper - Japanese garden --- Sepak-Easy is a dialogue system for Unity built with ease of use as a primary goal. When I got started in Unity, I was frustrated that although you can find third-party dialogue systems, they were packed with features making them appealing for commercial use, but making them way harder to get into. This system is simple to set-up, and great for more small-scale projects.

Features

  • Simple to set-up
    Just drag-and-drop the dialogue system into your scene.
