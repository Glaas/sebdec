High Rise is a innovative take on the match 3 genre. A relaxed casual puzzle experience. Match colourful cubes and watch them merge, grow and your 3D puzzle cityscape rise. Simple and engaging gameplay, that is easy to learn, but leaves a lot of space for your own tactics and personal improvement.

The game was initially developed by Florian Grolig, and I was hired as freelance to develop an update. I mostly worked on:

  • Online leaderboards
  • IAP
  • Gameplay tweaks
  • Undo system
