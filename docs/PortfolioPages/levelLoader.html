When working with scenes loadded additivily in Unity, it can be frustrating and tedious to load them individually, especially when iterating on levels. I was working on a 2d platformer consisting of many short scenes, which was a good use case for a custom tool.

Features

  • Simple to set-up Just drag-and-drop the level loader into your scene.
